# Privacy Policy for Freckle

**Last Updated:** November 20, 2025

## Introduction

Freckle ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal skin blemish tracking application that helps you monitor skin changes over time. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

**Important:** Freckle is designed with privacy in mind. All your personal data, including photos and notes, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal tracking data.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Health Information (Stored Locally Only)

The following data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers:

- **Photos:** Images of skin blemishes taken with your device camera
- **Blemish Information:**
  - Body location of tracked blemishes
  - Custom names and notes you add
  - Reference object measurements
  - Dates when photos were taken
  - Notification preferences and schedules
- **App Settings:**
  - Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
  - Notification settings
  - Onboarding completion status

**We do not have access to this data. It remains on your device.**

### 2. Technical & Analytics Data (Collected via Firebase)

We use Google Firebase to help us understand app usage and fix crashes:

- **Analytics Data:**
  - Device information (model, operating system version)
  - App usage statistics (screens viewed, features used)
  - Session duration and frequency
  - General usage patterns

- **Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics):**
  - App crash logs and error reports
  - Device state at time of crash
  - Stack traces for debugging

**Important:** Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics do **NOT** have access to your photos, blemish data, or personal health information.

### 3. Device Permissions

Freckle requires the following permissions:

- **Camera:** To take photos of skin blemishes for tracking purposes. Photos are stored locally on your device only.
- **Notifications:** To send you reminders to check and photograph tracked blemishes based on your chosen schedule.
- **Internet:** Required for Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics reporting only. Not used to transmit your personal health data.

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Data (Photos & Blemish Information)
- Stored exclusively on your device
- Used only to display your tracking history within the app
- Never uploaded to external servers
- Never shared with third parties

### Analytics & Crash Data
- To understand how users interact with the app
- To identify and fix bugs and crashes
- To improve app performance and features
- To make informed decisions about future updates

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties**, except:

- **Firebase (Google):** Technical analytics and crash data as described above
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, regulation, or legal process

**Your photos and blemish tracking data are never shared with anyone.**

## Data Storage & Security

### Local Storage
- All personal health data is stored in a local database on your device
- Protected by your device's security features (encryption, lock screen)
- You maintain full control over this data

### Firebase Data
- Analytics and crash data is transmitted securely over HTTPS
- Stored on Google's secure servers with industry-standard encryption
- Retained according to Firebase's data retention policies

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** View all your tracked blemishes and photos within the app
- **Delete:** Remove individual blemishes, photos, or all your data through the app
- **Export:** (Feature may be available in settings)
- **Control:** Disable notifications or analytics if desired

### How to Delete Your Data

**Local Data (Photos & Blemishes):**
- Delete individual blemishes within the app, or
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data, or
- Clear app data in your device settings

**Analytics Data:**
- Opt out of analytics by disabling app permissions
- Contact us to request deletion of analytics data associated with your device

## Children's Privacy

Freckle is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Third-Party Services

Freckle uses the following third-party services:

### Firebase (Google)
- **Services Used:** Analytics, Crashlytics
- **Purpose:** App analytics and crash reporting
- **Privacy Policy:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Firebase may collect device identifiers and usage information. Please review Google's privacy policy for more details.

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **Analytics Data:** Retained according to Firebase's retention policies (typically 2-14 months)
- **Crash Data:** Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes

## International Data Transfers

Analytics and crash data processed by Firebase may be transferred to and stored on servers in the United States and other countries. By using Freckle, you consent to these transfers.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice in the app
- Requiring acknowledgment of changes on first app launch after update

Continued use of Freckle after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
